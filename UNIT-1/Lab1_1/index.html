Meet Laufey

Early Life

Laufey (pronounced Lay-vay) was born on April 23, 1999.


She is Icelandic-Chinese, and has an identical twin sister named Junia.


She started playing piano at the age of 4 and the cello at the age of 8.



Laufey graduated from an Icelandic music school in 2018 where she studied signing for 2 years.


At the age of 15, Laufey performed as a cello soloist with the Iceland Symphony Orchestra.


She also participated in Iceland's Got Talent, where she was a finalist.

Career

Laufey debuted with her single "Street by Street in 2020, which charted at number one on Icelandic Radio.


She went on to release many more tracks. Many of these were written in her college dorm room at the Berklee College of Music.


Laufey performed at the London Jazz Festival in 2021, and also collaborated with London's Philharmonia Orchestra to release "Let You Break My Heart Again" later that year.


She released her debut album Everything I Know About Love in 2022! (this is my favorite)



Her newest release was her album Bewitched, which came out on September 8th, 2023.
